What is full stack developer?
               A full-stack developer is a developer or engineer who can build both the front end and the back end of a website. The front end (the parts of a website a user sees and interacts with) and the back end (the behind-the-scenes data storage and processing) require different skill sets.If you wonder how hard it is to become a full stack developer for a non-tech person, the answer is a full stack developer has a deep understanding of both front-end and back-end development.
